Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much does a dumpster rental cost near me?

Dumpster rental prices vary by state due to local disposal fees, fuel costs, and container availability. On average, prices range from $300 to $800. We offer competitive flat-rate pricing to ensure you get an affordable dumpster rental in your specific area.

2. What can I put in a roll off dumpster?

You can dispose of most household junk, furniture, yard waste, and construction debris in our bins. However, hazardous materials, tires, batteries, and wet paint are generally prohibited. We provide a full list of allowed items for your waste bin rental upon booking.

3. How long is the rental period?

Our standard rental period is generally 7 to 10 days. If you need the trash rental for a longer duration, simply let us know, and we can usually arrange a flexible extension for a small daily fee.

4. Can I put concrete in a regular dumpster?

YNo, heavy aggregates like concrete require specific concrete dumpsters near me to avoid weight overages. Mixing heavy materials in a standard rubbish bin rental can lead to safety issues and extra fees.

5. How do I choose the right size?

For small cleanouts, a 10 yard dumpster is usually sufficient. For kitchen remodels or roofing, a 20 yard dumpster is standard. Large construction projects typically require a 30 yard or 40 yard dumpster. We can assist with dumpster sizing.
